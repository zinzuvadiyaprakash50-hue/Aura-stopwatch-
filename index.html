Privacy Policy
1. Introduction
We prioritize your data security and individual privacy. This section outlines how data is handled entirely inside the application ecosystem.
2. Data Collection
All records, alarms, configurations, laps lists, and uploaded sounds are persisted 100% locally on your device via LocalStorage APIs. No system telemetry, data profiling, or server tracking mechanisms exist. Your data remains on your physical hardware.
3. Advertising and Analytics
Aura does not serve intrusive third-party advertisements or integrate tracking SDKs. It is optimized as a lightweight standalone tool.
4. Device Permissions
• Vibrate Haptics: Required to physically vibrate the smartphone when an alarm is actively ringing.
• Audio Playback: Required to process synth modules and local custom files offline.
5. Contact Point
For questions regarding implementation structures, security, or data handling, contact us at zinzuvadiyaprakash50@gmail.com
